How to Prepare Jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of long grain parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 4 fresh tomatoes, blended
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 2 scotch bonnets (optional), blended
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken or beef stock
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

    
  1. Heat oil in a pot, sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add tomato paste and fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Add blended tomato mixture, season with thyme, curry, and seasoning cubes. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Pour in stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add washed rice, stir, cover and cook on low heat.
  6. Stir occasionally to prevent burning. Add water if needed.
  7. Cook until rice is soft and flavorful. Serve hot!
