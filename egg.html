Grilled VeggieÂ Salad

This nutritious pasta dish is packed with grilled vegetables and tossed in a light herb dressing.

Ingredients

  • 200g whole wheat pasta
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and grill the bell pepper, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes until slightly charred.
  3. Add the cooked pasta and toss everything together.
  4. Season with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
