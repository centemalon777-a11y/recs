Lemon Roasted Chicken

A simple and flavorful lemon roasted chicken recipe with garlic and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and arrange lemon slices.
  4. Roast for 1 hour until golden and cooked through.
