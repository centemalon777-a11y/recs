Grilled Veggie Pasta

This nutritious pasta dish is packed with grilled vegetables and tossed in a light herb dressing.

Ingredients

  • - 250g pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • - 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • - ½ cup green peas
  • - 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (green, sliced)
  • - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • -Grated parmesan (optional)
  • - Fresh basil or parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook the pasta - Boil water with a pinch of salt. - Add pasta and cook according to the package instructions. - Drain and set aside (reserve ½ cup of pasta water).
  2. 2. Sauté the veggies - In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. - Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds. - Add broccoli, zucchini, peas, and bell pepper. Cook for 4–5 minutes. - Add spinach last and stir until wilted.
  3. 3. Combine pasta and veggies - Add cooked pasta to the veggie mix. - Pour in a bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen if needed. - Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. 4. Serve - Garnish with parmesan and fresh herbs. - Serve warm.
