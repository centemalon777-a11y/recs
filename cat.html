How to Cook Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add diced chicken or shrimp (if using) and cook until done.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice and stir everything together.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, scrambled eggs, salt, and pepper.
  7. Stir-fry for another 3–5 minutes until heated through.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
